My Nutri Scan ("we", "our", or "us") operates the My Nutri Scan mobile application (“App”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account or log in, we collect:

  • Email address (required for authentication)
  • Password (securely handled by Firebase Authentication)

We do not collect your name, phone number, physical address, or precise location.

Automatically Collected Data

The App may automatically collect non-personal data such as:

  • Device type and operating system
  • Crash logs and performance data (handled by Google Play Services)
  • Anonymous usage statistics

No personally identifiable information is included in this automated data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your email only to:

  • Create and authenticate your account
  • Allow you to log into the App securely

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information.

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication — for secure login
  • Google Play Services — for app stability and analytics

You can review Firebase’s privacy practices here: Firebase Privacy Policy.

4. Cookies

The App does not use cookies. Some third-party services (such as Firebase) may use similar tracking technologies for security and analytics purposes.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all associated personal data is permanently removed.

6. Data Deletion Rights

You may request account deletion at any time by contacting us at: nutriscanmy@gmail.com.

7. Security

We use industry-standard security methods. All authentication data is securely handled by Firebase and never stored locally on our servers.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
